📊 SuRIA Home 资助计划

📊 SuRIA Home Subsidy Program

💡 什么是 SuRIA Home？What is SuRIA Home?

这是马来西亚政府为鼓励住宅用户安装太阳能而推出的官方现金回扣计划。旨在帮助民众降低安装初创成本，实现长期省电。

This is an official cash rebate program launched by the Malaysian government to encourage residential users to install solar energy. It aims to help people reduce installation costs and achieve long-term electricity savings.

💬 政府支持： 💬 Government Support: 由永续能源发展机构 (SEDA) 和国能 (TNB) 联合推出 Jointly launched by SEDA (Suruhanjaya Tenaga Boleh Diperbaharui) and TNB

💰 您能获得多少回扣？How Much Rebate Can You Get?

💵 回扣标准

💵 Rebate Rate

  • 每安装 1 kWac 系统，可拿回 RM600 现金
  • For every 1 kWac system installed, get RM600 cash

🎯 回扣上限

🎯 Maximum Rebate

  • 最高 RM3,000 现金回扣（对应 5 kWac 及以上的系统）
  • Maximum RM3,000 cash rebate (for systems 5 kWac and above)
📈 示例： 📈 Example: • 3 kWac 系统 = RM1,800 | • 5 kWac 系统 = RM3,000 | • 7 kWac 系统 = RM3,000 • 3 kWac system = RM1,800 | • 5 kWac system = RM3,000 | • 7 kWac system = RM3,000

  • Citizenship: Malaysian citizen

  • System: Already installed Solar ATAP system

  • Limitation: One application per TNB account, no previous national solar subsidies

⚡ Solar ATAP 全新机制

⚡ Solar ATAP New Framework

🎯 什么是 Solar ATAP？What is Solar ATAP?

全称：Solar Accelerated Transition Action Programme

Full name: Solar Accelerated Transition Action Programme

自 2026 年 1 月 1 日起，大马政府正式启动的全新太阳能框架，全面取代了旧的 NEM 3.0 系统。

Starting January 1, 2026, Malaysia's new solar framework officially launched by the government, completely replacing the old NEM 3.0 system.

✨ 核心优势： ✨ Key Benefits:
  • 不再设"固定总配额"限制，更多家庭能安装太阳能
  • No fixed total quota limit, more homes can install solar
  • 计费更透明，回扣更直接
  • More transparent billing, direct rebates
  • 自发自用，多余回扣，机制更合理
  • Self-use priority, excess sold back, more rational mechanism

🔄 核心运作逻辑：「自发自用」Core Logic: "Self-Consumption First"

1️⃣ 白天直接免费用

1️⃣ Free Daytime Usage

太阳能板发电后，直接供您家里的冷气、冰箱等电器使用。这部分电完全免费，帮您省下高昂的 TNB 电费。

Solar panels generate electricity for your AC, refrigerator, and appliances directly. This portion is completely free, saving you expensive TNB fees.

2️⃣ 多余电量卖给 TNB

2️⃣ Sell Excess to TNB

如果您家里没人或用电少，用不完的电会自动输送回 TNB 电网。

If your home is empty or uses less electricity, excess power is automatically sent back to TNB grid.

3️⃣ 电费单直接扣除

3️⃣ Direct Bill Deduction

输送回电网的电量会转化为电费信用 (Credits)，直接在您当月的 TNB 电费单里扣除。住宅用户直接抵扣每度电的零售电价成本。

Returned electricity converts to bill credits (Credits) deducted directly from your monthly TNB bill. Residential users get full retail price credit.

⚠️ 重要：每月结算，不滚存 ⚠️ Important: Monthly Settlement, No Carryover

太阳能回扣是在当月电费单中直接扣除。如果发出的电太多，把当月电费扣到 RM0 还有剩余，剩余的额度不能带到下个月，也不会换成现金发放。

Solar credits are deducted directly from your current month's bill. If excess remains after reducing bill to RM0, it cannot carry to next month and won't be paid as cash.

💡 Spower 的专业建议（Right-Sizing）： 💡 Spower Professional Advice (Right-Sizing):

在新的 ATAP 机制下，系统并非"越大越好"，而是需要根据您家庭白天的实际用电比例进行精准量身设计，才能让投资回报率（ROI）最大化。我们的专家团队将为您免费评估和设计最优方案。

Under ATAP, bigger is not always better. Systems should be precisely sized to your daytime usage to maximize ROI. Our expert team will free assess and design the optimal solution.

📦 住宅安装容量限制Residential Installation Capacity Limits

  • 单相电表（Single-Phase）：最高可安装 5 kW
  • Single-Phase Meter: Maximum 5 kW installation
  • 三相电表（Three-Phase）：最高可安装 15 kW
  • Three-Phase Meter: Maximum 15 kW installation
📊 一般家庭足够： 📊 Typical Household Sufficient:

大多数马来西亚家庭都是单相电表，5 kW 容量足以满足日常需求并最大化 ROI。

Most Malaysian homes have single-phase meters. 5 kW capacity is sufficient for daily needs and maximizes ROI.

📜 合约期限与权益Contract Duration & Rights

  • 10 年合约期：与 TNB 签署的 ATAP 发电回扣合约期限为 10 年
  • 10-Year Contract: ATAP generation rebate contract with TNB is 10 years
  • 10 年后：系统依然属于您并可以继续免费发电供家里自用
  • After 10 Years: System remains yours, can continue generating free electricity for home use
  • 系统寿命：现代太阳能系统寿命长达 25+ 年，质保通常 10-25 年
  • System Lifespan: Modern solar systems last 25+ years with warranties typically 10-25 years
✅ 长期价值： ✅ Long-Term Value:

这不是租赁，您完全拥有这个系统。10 年合约结束后，您仍然享受大约 15+ 年的完全免费用电。

This is ownership, not leasing. After 10 years, you still enjoy 15+ years of completely free electricity.

Q: 我的房屋面积很小，适合装太阳能吗？

Q: Is solar suitable for small houses?

A: 通过精准的 Right-Sizing 规划，即使小面积住宅也可以获得良好的 ROI。建议先进行屋顶勘察与用电分析。Spower 提供免费的专业评估。

A: Through precise Right-Sizing planning, even small homes can achieve good ROI. We recommend free roof assessment and electricity analysis first. Spower provides professional evaluation at no cost.

Q: 系统投运后多久能申请 SuRIA Home 回扣？

Q: When can I apply for SuRIA Home rebate after installation?

A: 通常系统成功投运并在 TNB 系统注册后即可申请，具体时间请咨询您的安装商或 TNB 客服。Spower 提供全程协助。

A: Usually after system is operational and registered in TNB system. Contact your installer or TNB customer service for specifics. Spower assists throughout the process.

Q: 下雨天或晚上有发电吗？

Q: Does it generate electricity on rainy days or at night?

A: 太阳能板在阴天会有少量发电，但效率大幅下降（约 10-25%）。晚上无法发电，此时需从电网供电。

A: Solar panels generate minimal power on cloudy days (10-25% efficiency). They don't generate at night; you draw from the grid. We design systems accounting for this.

Q: 太阳能系统需要维护吗？

Q: Does the solar system need maintenance?

A: 建议 1-3 年清洁一次面板（如果很脏）。大多数系统故障率很低，质保通常为 10-25 年。Spower 提供售后维护服务。

A: Clean panels every 1-3 years if very dirty. Most systems have low failure rates with 10-25 year warranties. Spower offers after-sales maintenance.

Q: How long does installation take?

A: Typically 2-4 weeks (including survey, design, procurement, installation, grid approval). Can be expedited if needed. Spower project manager tracks throughout.
